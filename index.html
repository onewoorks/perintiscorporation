PERINTIS CORPORATION

We provide total logistic solution in marine cargo handling with efficient and effectivevalue for money.

Perintis Corporation Sdn Bhd established 2009 provides ship to ship cargo transfer services (STS) at our dedicated gazetted STS area at Johore Bahru Port Limit in Tanjung Pelepas, Johor MALAYSIA. We aim to provide the best transportation, harbour services, through our involved, dedicated and skilled people, delivering excellent customer services. We work to achieve this mission through honesty, integrity and a true desire to exceed customer expectations. We are committed to the highest level of personal, equipment and the environment.
